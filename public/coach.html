HARRY COCHRANE

PERSONAL TRAINER

I've been involved in fitness and sport my entire life. From competitive rugby to strength training, I've experienced firsthand the transformative power of dedicated physical training.

After years of training myself and helping friends reach their goals, I decided to make it official. I qualified as a personal trainer because I believe everyone deserves access to quality coaching - not just elite athletes.

My approach is simple: build sustainable habits through consistent, intelligent training. No fads, no shortcuts - just proven methods that work. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced lifter, I'll meet you where you are and help you get to where you want to be.
